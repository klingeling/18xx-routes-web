关于

18xx 路线计算器是 18xx 系列桌游的辅助工具 (例如1846: 美国中西部铁路竞速), to make route calculations easier and faster in-game. For that reason, they do not implement the entire ruleset; just enough to allow you to run routes, and some checks to prevent you from entering illegal board states.

Place tiles by clicking on the board, then selecting a tile and orientation from the resulting modal. Only legal tiles and orientations are displayed. Railroads and private companies can be added via the tabs to the right of the board (or below it, if you have a very narrow screen). This game state is automatically saved in your browser, and loaded next time you visit.

To calculate a route, click the Calculate button and select a railroad. Only railroads with at least 1 train can be picked. Each route is displayed in text to the right on the map, and drawn on the map.

来源

The source of this web app is available on GitHub, including bug and improvement tracking and a changelist. The source for the route finding algorithm is also on GitHub, and is available as a standalone Python package.

图像来源

The tile images are from Blackwater Station. The 1889 board is Carthaginian's redesign with coordinates overlaid. The 1846 board is copyright 2016 GMT Games, LLC. It is a modified form of the version linked from their 1846 game page.
